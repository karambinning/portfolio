// HOW I BUILD

OPERATING PRINCIPLES

Four rules keep a one-person estate of a dozen products from collapsing — and they're the real differentiator.

01

Real data or nothing

Every product runs on live, real sources — never mock data. Empty states show structure, not fakery. The honesty is the brand.

02

Non-destructive evolution

Extend, never delete. Backups everywhere, version DBs to v134, live build journals. Old shells are archived, not erased.

03

One aesthetic, everywhere

The KAM terminal skin unifies a dozen unrelated products — cyan for the estate, gold for law, red for the trading floor.

04

Be scientific

Measure with gameable-resistant metrics — CLV, Brier, verified-rate. Audit your own work honestly, even when the verdict is "this was theatre."
